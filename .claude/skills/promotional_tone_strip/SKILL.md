# Skill: Promotional Tone Strip

## Purpose
Remove ad-like language and exaggeration.

## Target Words
boasts, vibrant, stunning, nestled, breathtaking, renowned, groundbreaking, rich (figurative)

## Rules
- Replace with factual descriptions
- Remove hype language
- Keep tone grounded

## Prompt Mode
"Remove every promotional adjective from this text (boasts, vibrant, stunning, nestled, breathtaking, renowned, groundbreaking, rich used figuratively). Replace with factual descriptions or rewrite the sentence."

## Goal
Make writing feel real, not like marketing fluff.
