# Skill: AI Vocabulary Scrub

## Purpose
Replace overly complex or AI-typical vocabulary with simple, direct language.

## Replace Words
delve, leverage, tapestry, landscape, pivotal, underscore, showcase, testament, vibrant, crucial, align, foster, enhance

## Rules
- Rewrite sentences using simpler wording
- Keep meaning the same
- Avoid sounding academic or inflated

## Prompt Mode
"Find and replace these words: delve, leverage, tapestry, landscape, pivotal, underscore, showcase, testament, vibrant, crucial, align, foster, enhance. Rewrite each sentence using simpler, more direct language."

## Goal
Make writing sound natural and human.
