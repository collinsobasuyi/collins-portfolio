# Skill: Clean Punctuation

## Purpose
Remove artificial punctuation that makes writing feel AI-generated.

## Rules
- Remove all em dashes (—)
- Remove all double dashes (--)
- Replace with:
  - commas
  - full stops
  - or restructure sentence

## Prompt Mode
"Remove every em dash (—) from this text. Replace each one with a comma, a period, or a restructured sentence. The result should read naturally without dramatic pauses."

## Goal
Keep writing natural and easy to read.
