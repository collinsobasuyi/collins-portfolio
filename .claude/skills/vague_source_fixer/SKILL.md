# Skill: Vague Source Fixer

## Purpose
Remove weak or vague attribution.

## Target
- experts say
- studies show
- some argue
- observers note
- industry reports

## Rules
- Replace with a real source OR
- Rewrite sentence to state claim directly

## Prompt Mode
"Find every vague attribution in this text (experts say, studies show, some argue, observers note, industry reports). Replace with a real source or rewrite the sentence to state the claim directly."

## Goal
Make statements confident and clear.
